  • WHAT IS NftLendo?

    NftLendo is the leading liquidity protocol for NFTs. We allow NFT owners to use the assets (NFTs) they own to access the liquidity they need by receiving secured wETH, DAI, and USDC loans from liquidity providers, peer-to-peer, in a completely trustless manner. NFT liquidity providers use NftLendo to earn attractive yields or — in the case of loan defaults — to have a chance at obtaining NFTs at a steep discount to their market value.

  • WHAT IS NftLendo's VISION?

    NftLendo's vision is to build a fully decentralized, permissionless, user-owner public utility, supporting the seamless financialization of NFT based economies through innovative mechanisms and highly user-friendly applications.

  • WHY DO COLLECTORS AND ARTIST LOVE NftLendo?

    The ability to access liquidity against their NFTs without selling the asset gives unprecedented financial flexibility to NFT holders, especially if they have a large percentage of their portfolio locked up in these illiquid assets. A few examples of what the liquidity obtained via NftLendo can be used for include:

    1. Serving immediate liquidity needs (e.g. covering margin positions).
    2. Taking advantage of short-term investment opportunities (e.g. high-yield liquidity mining or NFT flips).
    3. Taking advantage of long-term investment opportunities (e.g. buying real estate; long-term loans is now supported in NftLendo V2).
    4. Delaying a planned sale of an NFT for more opportune market conditions.
    5. Delaying a planned sale of an NFT to defer potential capital gains tax.
    6. Financing ‘real life’ needs without having to sell valuable assets.
