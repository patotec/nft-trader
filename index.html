    What Is NFT

    An NFT (non-fungible token) is a unique digital item stored on a blockchain. NFTs can represent almost anything, and serve as a digital record of ownership.

    How To Buy NFT

    In order to buy an NFT, you'll need a crypto wallet and cryptocurrency (or, in some cases, just a credit or debit card). Using OpenSea, you can buy items listed for sale instantly, bid in auctions, or make offers on any NFTs.

    How To Create NFT

    Creating an NFT on OpenSea is intuitive and easy. You just need to decide what the purpose and genre of your collection is, set up a crypto wallet, choose a blockchain, and list your NFT(s).
